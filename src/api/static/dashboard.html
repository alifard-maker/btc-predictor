How to read this dashboard

This page helps you decide whether BTC finishes above or below its price at the start of each 15-minute Eastern Time slot (:00, :15, :30, :45). It does not place trades. Refresh is automatic every 5 seconds.

Your price at t=0 (top box)

t=0 is the Kalshi KXBTC15M Target price — the BRTI 60-second average in the minute before slot open. Now updates every second from live CF Benchmarks BRTI (requires KALSHI_PRIVATE_KEY on Railway). Without it, Coinbase is used as an approximate live feed; P&L is always vs Kalshi t=0.

Current 15-minute slot

  • Reference (t=0) — Starting price for this slot. UP means close above this; DOWN means close below.
  • UP / DOWN % — Model estimate for where price lands at slot close vs t=0.
  • LONG / SHORT / NO TRADE — LONG if UP ≥ ~57%; SHORT if UP ≤ ~43%; otherwise sit out.
  • Expected move — Rough $ move implied by recent volatility (not a target).
  • Confidence — How far the model is from 50/50 (higher = more decisive).
  • Live now — Live CF Benchmarks BRTI (Kalshi passthrough).
  • Kalshi YES mid — Market-implied UP probability on KXBTC15M (cents).

Signal breakdown

Ingredients behind the UP/DOWN bars — not separate trade signals. Green bar = leans UP for the next slot; red = leans DOWN; length = strength.

Short-term (1h)
1h momentum, slot return, and how many of the last four 15m bars closed up.
Medium (4h)
Four-hour trend on 15m candles.
Regime (12h)
Twelve-hour move; extended moves may be faded (mean-reversion nudge).
Context
Volume vs 4h average, RSI (14), and distance from 12h VWAP.

System status

Health of the API, scheduler, and Kalshi BRTI feed. Green dots = OK.

Live position · intra-slot exit

If you bet at open, use HOLD / TAKE PROFIT / CUT LOSS. If the open was NO TRADE, the monitor watches the slot and may suggest a LATE LONG/SHORT only after several minutes of consistent price action (not instant flips).

WATCH
No bet at open — collecting slot history; shows outlook after ~2 min.
LATE LONG / LATE SHORT
Conservative mid-slot entry when move vs t=0, time on side of ref, and momentum align (needs ~4+ min elapsed, 3+ min left).
HOLD
Position still on track; no urgent action.
TAKE PROFIT
In profit and move may be exhausted — consider locking gains.
CUT LOSS
Losing with momentum against you — consider exiting; includes reassessed UP/DOWN for time left in the slot.
FLIP LONG / FLIP SHORT
Open bet losing past stop and reassessment strongly favors the other side — exit open leg, bet opposite. One flip per slot; after that only HOLD/TP/CUT on the flipped side.
  • Ref (t=0) — Kalshi BRTI target at slot open.
  • Now — Live BRTI index.
  • Move vs t=0 — Dollar difference between live price and t=0 (e.g. +$50 if BTC is $50 above the slot open).
  • Time left — Countdown to slot close.

Calibration

Three Brier scores: slot-open (prob at t=0), 2nd Chance (reassessment at t+4min), late entry (first LATE signal), and flip (after losing open). Lower is better. All-time stats persist across epoch resets.

Recent slots: reference at t=0, UP probability, signal, and actual outcome once the slot resolves.
